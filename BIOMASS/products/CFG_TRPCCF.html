detection rule

This product definition is applicable if a product matches the following rule:

substr(0, 4, filename()) == "BIO_" and
substr(9, 10, filename()) == "CFG_TRPCCF" and
substr(56, 4, filename()) == ".EOF" and
exists(/Earth_Observation_File/Earth_Observation_Header/Fixed_Header/File_Type) and
at(/Earth_Observation_File/Earth_Observation_Header/Fixed_Header/File_Type,
